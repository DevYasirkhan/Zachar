About Zachar Law

Since 1996, Zachar Law Firm has been securing favorable settlements and large verdicts for our clients. We have a commitment to protecting the rights of personal injury victims in our community, and winning for you the maximum settlement possible. At Zachar Law Firm, we offer the individual attention of a small firm with the resources and trial experience of a large practice.

Our founding attorney, Christopher J. Zachar, has nearly three decades of experience practicing law in the state of Arizona. A skilled litigator, Zachar has dedicated his career to building a specialized practice in personal injury and wrongful death law. He has earned the trust of his clients, as and the respect of the insurance companies, judges and his peers along the way

When you or a loved one has been injured in an accident, we know how important it is to speak to a lawyer who understands your frustrations. That’s why, whether in the courthouse or at the negotiating table, we’re prepared to fight on your behalf. We are Zachar Law Firm. We are compassionate attorneys, providing exceptional results.

At Zachar Law, personal injury and wrongful death law is what we do. In fact, it is all we do.

Meet Christopher Zachar

Chris Zachar is a Phoenix-based personal injury trial lawyer. A graduate of Arizona State University (1989) and the University of Arizona College of Law (1992), he has been practicing law in the state since 1992. As a Board Certified Specialist in Personal Injury and Wrongful Death law, Chris is part of a very small group of attorneys—less than 2% statewide—who have achieved this specialized recognition by the Arizona State Bar. His practice includes all city, county and Arizona state courts, as well the United States District Court, the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals and the US Supreme Court.

A skillful litigator, Chris has earned a reputation for obtaining very favorable settlements and large verdicts for his clients in matters involving personal injury and wrongful death. Settlements can often be reached out of court, but when this isn’t possible, Chris brings the trial experience needed to argue and win your case in front of a jury.
